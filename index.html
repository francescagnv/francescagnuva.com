About

I'm Francesca — a multidisciplinary creative based wherever curiosity takes me. I work at the crossroads of design, strategy, and technology, building products and experiences that are both beautiful and meaningful.

My background spans product design, research, and entrepreneurship. I believe the best work happens when rigorous thinking meets genuine care for the people you're building for.

When I'm not designing systems or writing, you'll find me exploring new cities, deep in a book, or cooking elaborate meals for friends.
